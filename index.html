Vremya i Steklo (Russian: Время и Стекло "Time and Glass") is a Ukrainian pop duo formed in Ukraine by Potap. The group consists of Aleksey Zavgorodniy and Nadya Dorofeeva. Aleksey and Nadya were the participants and guests of large-scale music events, such as, the "Golden Gramophone Award", the "Crimea Music Fest", the "Slavianski Bazaar", "YUNA", "RU.TV Music Award" and others.Despite the current hostile Ukrainian-Russian relations since the 2014 Russian annexation of Crimea Vremya I Steklo continue to tour in Russia.
